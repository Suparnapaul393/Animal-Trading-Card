Giant Panda

When giant pandas are born, they are tiny, blind, and pink, with few hairs.

  • Scientific Name: Cailuropoda melanoleuca
  • Average Length: 60 – 90 cm
  • Average Lifespan: 14 and 20 years
  • Habitat: China’s Minshan Mountains

About 300 giant pandas live in captivity; no more than 3,000 exist in the wild. The endangered species is not uncommon in pop culture, though, evidenced by animated films such as DreamWorks’ Kung Fu Panda, news stories every time a cub is born in a zoo, and stuffed animals on beds the world over. Vegetarian bears with black-rimmed eyes and fluffy coats, giant pandas are as peculiar as they are rare.
